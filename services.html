SERVICES WE PROVIDE

EXPERIENCE OUR EXCEPTIONAL
SERVICES ON YOUR SHIP

We take satisfaction in collaborating closely with our customers as a reliable partner, valuing their work, and assisting them in achieving their objectives. We can give specific solutions that are tailored to our client's demands because of our deep experience in the Egyptian market. Also, because of our flexibility and dedication to helping you reach your goals, we are able to provide the best services available, including:

Technical Ship Management

  • Meeting the requirements of the flag state.
  • Complying with the International Safety Management (ISM) Code and International Ship and Port Facility Security (ISPS) Code.
  • Providing competent personnel to supervise vessel maintenance and efficiency.
  • Arranging and supervising dry dockings, repairs, alterations, and maintenance for the vessel.
  • Meeting the requirements and recommendations of the classification society.
  • As necessary, appointing surveyors and technical consultants .
  • Arranging for the sampling and testing of bunkers.

Crewing Management

We collaborate with manning agencies worldwide to acquire technically proficient and culturally sensitive seafarers for their managed vessels.


Our partners can take advantage of the following crewing services:


  • Crew recruiting.
  • Making plans for the crew's deployment and return to the ship.
  • Crew training and professional advancement.
  • Payroll

Chandlery And Ship Supplies

In Egypt, we have provided supplies to a variety of local business sectors.


The ship chandlery services include:


  • Providing Lubricants, Coatings and Bunkers
  • Provision and Bonded Stores
  • Deck and Engine Supplies
  • Safety Equipment

Commercial Ship Management

  • Securing employment aboard a vessel under a charter or a contract of carriage.

  • Managing risk and obtaining proper marine insurance.

Ship Rental Services

Ship rental services are offered to clients who wish to charter vessels at a favorable rate.

We work closely with clients to understand their specific requirements and provide customized solutions.

Using our expertise and experience in the maritime industry, we strive to provide seamless and hassle-free ship rental services for our clients.

Shipping Agency

  • Providing a berth for the incoming ship.
  • Arrangements for the pilot and tugboats.
  • Preparation of documents for customs and harbor services.
  • Provision of the goods, transport, and handling.
  • Setting up contacts with stevedores.
  • Collecting freights, and cargoes.
  • Contacting shippers and receivers of the goods.
