WHO WE ARE

Motion Marine Services is an Alexandria-based ship management company established in 2017, with a team of experienced professionals offering a complete range of integrated ship management and technical services.

The company is committed to providing the highest levels of quality, safety, and reliability in the maritime industry, and is certified with ISO 2015 -9001.

Also, the company has been registered with the International Maritime Organization as a general services supplier.

As a trusted partner in maritime logistics and warehousing, Motion Marine Services navigates the complexity of international and domestic logistics and warehousing solutions.

OUR STORY

Motion Marine Services is a well-experienced team of industry professionals who have been the preferred marine company for customers over the years.

As one of the leading marine suppliers in Egypt for the past five years, Motion Marine Services has worked with some of the biggest companies in the field, building a strong reputation for reliability and quality.
